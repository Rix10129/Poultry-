How It Works — The Simple Version

🌐
It opens in your browser

Like a website — but it works on your own PC even without internet

💾
Your data stays on your PC

Nothing is shared online. Your business data is private and secure.

⚠ Your system has already been set up for you

Your provider has already installed and configured everything on this PC. You do not need to install anything. Just follow the daily steps below.

Every Day — Opening the Software

1
Turn on your PC and wait about 2–3 minutes

The software starts itself automatically in the background. You do not need to open or click anything during this time — just let the PC load completely.

2
Double-click "Open Poultry Vet" on your Desktop

This icon was placed on your desktop during setup. It opens the software directly in your browser.

🌐
Open Poultry Vet

Double-click this icon

3
Log in with your email and password

Your browser opens to the login page. Enter your email and password and click Sign In.

🎉 That's it — you're in!

No terminals. No waiting for programs to load. Just double-click and work.

💡 If the page shows an error when you first click

The software is still loading in the background. Wait 1–2 more minutes, then refresh the page (press F5). This sometimes happens right after turning on the PC.

Closing the Software

✅
Correct way

Just close the browser tab or window. Your data is saved automatically — nothing is lost.

✅
Shutting down the PC

Just shut down Windows normally. You do not need to do anything special before shutting down.

💡 You don't need to "stop" the software

Unlike the old version, you don't need to run any stop commands. Just close the browser and shut down your PC when you're done.

Backups — Do This Every Week

🚨 This is very important — please read

If your computer is stolen, gets a virus, or stops working, a backup is the ONLY way to recover your data. It takes less than 1 minute. Do it every week.

1
Double-click "Backup Poultry Vet" on your Desktop
💾
Backup Poultry Vet

Double-click this icon

A small popup will appear saying "Backup saved successfully!"

2
Copy the backup to a USB drive

Open the Poultry- → docker → backups folder. You will see files with today's date. Copy the entire backups folder to a USB drive.

3
Keep the USB drive somewhere safe

Store it somewhere away from the computer — at home, in a drawer, or with a trusted person. If the PC is stolen, your USB drive saves everything.

If Something Goes Wrong

❓ The "Open Poultry Vet" icon is missing from my Desktop
Open your browser (Chrome or Edge) and type http://localhost:3000 in the address bar and press Enter.
❓ The page shows an error or doesn't load
Wait 2–3 minutes after turning on the PC, then try again. If it still doesn't work, restart the PC and wait 3 minutes before clicking the icon.
❓ I forgot my password
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You will receive an email with a link to set a new password.
❓ I want to add a staff member
Log in → click Users in the left menu → click Add User. Enter their name, email, and role. They log in the same way you do.
❓ The software is running slowly
Close other programs and extra browser tabs. Restart the PC — this usually fixes slowness.
❓ The backup says "failed"
The software may still be starting up. Wait 3 minutes after turning on the PC, then try the backup again.
❓ The PC was replaced or reinstalled — can I get my data back?
Yes — contact your system provider and give them the backup file from your USB drive. They will restore everything.
