How to Set Up & Use Your Software
Simple step-by-step guide for first-time setup
✅ What this software does for your business

Manage your sales, purchases, stock, customers, accounts, and reports — all in one place. Works on your PC even without internet. Your data stays on your own computer, safe and private.

1
Download one free program first

The Poultry Vet System needs a helper program called Docker Desktop to run on your computer. It is completely free and you only install it once.

Open this website in your browser
docker.com/products/docker-desktop
Click the big blue "Download for Windows" button
⏱ This download is about 500 MB

On a slow connection it may take 10–20 minutes to download. You only do this once.

2
Install the helper program
①

Find the downloaded file in your Downloads folder — it is called Docker Desktop Installer.exe

②

Double-click it to open the installer

③

Click OK on everything it asks — just keep clicking Next / OK / Accept

④

Your computer may restart — that is normal, let it restart

⑤

After restarting, a whale icon 🐳 will appear in the bottom-right corner of your screen (near the clock). Wait for it to stop moving — when it is still, Docker is ready.

💡 Think of it like this

Docker Desktop is like the engine of a car. You don't use it directly — it just needs to be running in the background so the Poultry Vet software can work. Every time you start your PC, open Docker Desktop first.

3
Copy the Poultry Vet folder to your computer

Your system provider will give you a USB drive or send you the software folder. Copy the entire Poultry- folder to your computer — for example into your Documents folder.

✅ Make sure you have these files inside the docker folder

📄 start.bat — this is what you double-click to open the software
📄 stop.bat — this closes the software at end of day
📄 backup.bat — this saves a copy of all your data

4
Start the software for the first time
⏱ First time only: takes 5 to 15 minutes

The very first time you start, the software sets itself up automatically. After that, it starts in under 1 minute every time. Just be patient on this first start.

①

Make sure the 🐳 whale icon is visible and still in the taskbar (Docker is running)

②

Open the Poultry- → docker folder

③

Double-click start.bat

④

A black window opens and shows some text — this is normal, just wait

⑤

Your browser (Chrome/Edge) will open automatically with the login page

💡 If the browser doesn't open automatically

Open Chrome or Edge yourself and type this in the address bar:

http://localhost:3000
5
Create your account
①

On the login page, click "Create Account" or go to:

http://localhost:3000/register
②

Fill in: your company name, your name, email address, and a password

③

Click Create Account

④

You will see a message: "Registration submitted — awaiting admin approval"

⑤

Call or WhatsApp your system provider — they will approve your account within a few minutes. You will also get an email confirmation.

⑥

Once approved, go back to http://localhost:3000 and log in with your email and password

🎉 You are ready!

After logging in, you will see your dashboard. Start by adding your products in Inventory, then add your customers, and you're good to go.

Every Day — How to Open and Close
🌅 Morning — Opening
1. Open Docker Desktop
2. Wait for 🐳 to stop moving
3. Double-click start.bat
4. Browser opens — you're ready!
🌙 Evening — Closing
1. Double-click stop.bat
2. Wait for the black window to close
3. Now shut down your PC normally
⚠ Don't just close the browser — run stop.bat first
💡 Your data is always safe

Even if the power goes out or you close the software without running stop.bat — your data is never lost. It is saved on your PC automatically.

Backups — Take One Every Week
🚨 This is the most important habit

If your computer is stolen, catches a virus, or stops working — your backup is the only way to get your data back. It takes less than 1 minute. Please do this every week.

①

Make sure the software is running (start.bat has been run)

②

Double-click backup.bat

③

A black window opens and closes automatically — the backup is done

④

Go into the docker → backups folder — you will see a file with today's date

⑤

Copy the backups folder to a USB drive and keep the USB drive somewhere safe (not next to the PC)

Recommended schedule:
Daily

Run backup.bat at the end of each working day

Weekly

Copy the backups folder to your USB drive

Monthly

Copy to a second USB drive. Keep one at home, one at the shop.

Something Not Working? Check Here First
❓ The software won't start / start.bat gives an error
Docker Desktop is probably not open. Find it in your Start Menu, open it, wait for the 🐳 whale to stop moving, then try start.bat again.
❓ The page shows an error or just loads forever
Wait 2–3 more minutes and refresh the page. After a PC restart, the first load takes extra time.
❓ I closed the browser — where is my software?
Just open Chrome or Edge and go to: http://localhost:3000 — the software is still running.
❓ I forgot my password
Go to http://localhost:3000/forgot-password and enter your email. You will receive a link to reset your password.
❓ The software is running slowly
Close other programs and extra browser tabs. The software needs some memory to run smoothly. Restarting the PC often fixes slowness.
❓ I want another staff member to use the software
Log in → go to Users in the menu → click Add User. Enter their name, email, and role. They log in at the same address: http://localhost:3000
